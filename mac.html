Keep your device on the network. Earn real value while it's idle.

Any laptop with 16 GB of RAM can run Hermes Llama 8B — the model the network primarily serves. What you contribute overnight offsets what you use during the day, or cashes out in USD.

Numbers are illustrative and assume ~60% utilization while idle. Live rates publish from gateway.teale.com.
