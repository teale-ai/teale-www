How it works

One app. Three things happen.

1

Install Teale

One command. Runs as a menu bar app. Detects your hardware, downloads a model that fits.

2

Use AI for free

Chat, code, create. Models run locally on your Mac. No internet, no account, no limits.

3

Your Mac helps others

When you're idle, your Mac serves requests from the network. That's what keeps your AI free.

The deal: you contribute, you use AI free.

You use AI maybe 8–12 hours a day. The other 12–16 hours, your Mac is idle. Teale uses that idle time to serve inference for others on the network. In exchange, your usage is free. The more people who join, the more supply there is, the better it works for everyone. No subscription. No corporation taking a cut. Just people sharing compute.

For developers
