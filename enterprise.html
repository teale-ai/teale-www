How it works

Enterprise-grade compute from distributed consumer hardware. No GPUs to provision.

1

Dedicated Capacity

We allocate a private cluster of Apple Silicon nodes to your workload. No shared tenancy. Guaranteed throughput and latency SLAs.

2

Secure Execution

All data is encrypted end-to-end. Computation is sharded across nodes so no single node sees your full dataset. Zero-knowledge architecture.

3

Managed Infrastructure

No GPUs to provision. No Kubernetes to manage. Our orchestration layer handles scheduling, fault tolerance, and auto-scaling. You get an API.

Teale's orchestration layer distributes workloads across thousands of Apple Silicon nodes connected via a global P2P network. Each node runs in a secure sandbox with cryptographic identity verification. The result: cloud-scale compute from distributed consumer hardware, at a fraction of the cost.
